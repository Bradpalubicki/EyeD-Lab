Phase 1

Centralized Billing Gates

Our central system automatically scrubs ALL incoming claims. No human guesswork.

The Gatekeeper Rule

If a chart is coded as a Level 4 visit, the system checks for two things:

1
The provider's .ECW_DATA_REVIEW text stamp — proves the doctor actually reviewed outside records
2
An active Particle Health API transaction timestamp — proves the system actually pulled records for this patient today

Both match? Claim drops cleanly to the payer. Either missing? Claim is held. Zero risk of unbacked claims reaching clearinghouse.

Phase 2

Behavioral Health Safety

The clinical risk: TRT and hypertension medications do NOT mix well with outside stimulant prescriptions (like Adderall). Patients often forget to disclose what outside psychiatrists prescribe them.

  • The tool: Providers must scan Particle's external pharmacy logs to check for hidden stimulant scripts before ordering new drugs.
  • The revenue loop: Tracking these interactions unlocks Collaborative Care Model (CoCM) billing codes worth $63 to $131 per month per patient.

Phase 3

Remote Patient Monitoring

  • The Apple Watch upgrade: No shipping expensive clinical hardware. If the patient owns an Apple Watch (Series 9+), pair it directly to eCW via HealthKit.

  • Staff action: The dashboard flags drop-offs. If a patient hasn't synced in 4 days — text them immediately.
