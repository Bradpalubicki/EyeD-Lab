The Problem

32,000 Americans die abroad every year.
Most from treatable conditions.

The #1 cause of preventable death in medical emergencies abroad is unknown patient history — allergies, medications, prior conditions. Foreign clinicians are treating Americans blind. Travel Guard pays the evacuation bill. EyeD ID eliminates the reason for evacuation.
