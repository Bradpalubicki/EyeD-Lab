Operational Infrastructure

The Centralized Hub absorbs all tracking and compliance work. Volume spikes at individual clinics don't affect floor staff — the Hub scales by adding remote care coordinators, not on-site hires.

Enterprise Risk Management

Automated billing scrubber enforces API timestamp matching against provider documentation macros. No Particle payload match = claim held automatically. Zero risk of unbacked claims reaching clearinghouse.
