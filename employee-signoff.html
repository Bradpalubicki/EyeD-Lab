Role-Specific Compliance Commitments

Check all items that apply to your role. Initial each section you are responsible for.

Front Desk / Intake Personnel
I will ensure that every check-in patient successfully signs the health information exchange (HIE) consent screen prior to updating their status to "Arrived."
I will not bypass the consent tracking modules under any circumstances.
If a patient asks about the consent, I will explain: "This safely searches medical networks for your allergies and past prescriptions so your doctor can treat you safely today."
Clinical Providers (MD, DO, NP, PA)
I understand that E/M code upgrades (99204/99214) require true, active medical decision-making data analysis — not boilerplate documentation.
I will review the Particle Health "External Documents" portal at point-of-care before every patient encounter.
I will append the .ECW_DATA_REVIEW macro strictly to qualifying clinical charts where I have genuinely reviewed external records and used them in my clinical decision-making.
I understand that misuse of the macro (applying it without genuine record review) constitutes billing fraud and will result in immediate disciplinary action.
Care Hub Tracking Personnel (RPM / BHI)
I understand that RPM device codes (CPT 99454) require the system ledger to display data transmission on 16 or more individual days within the calendar month.
I will utilize the embedded eCW stopwatch tool to document the required 20 minutes of discrete care tracking for CPT 99457 and CPT 99484.
I will not estimate time manually or count general office visit time toward the 20-minute discrete threshold.
If a patient has not synced their device in 4+ days, I will contact them immediately to prompt a manual sync.

By signing below, I acknowledge that I have received, reviewed, and fully understand the Total Men's Primary Care Operational Manual regarding the eClinicalWorks (eCW) and Particle Health integration. I understand that adhering to these workflows is vital to maintaining our clinical safety standards, protecting our clinical footprint from insurance audits, and guaranteeing compliant reimbursement loops.
