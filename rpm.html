Compliance Validation Logic

99454: 16-Day Transmission Rule

Patient's device must stream physiologic data (BP, glucose, SpO2, HR) on at least 16 separate days per 30-day billing period. Below 16 days = claim held automatically. Apple Watch qualifies with specific model documentation (Series 9/10/11/Ultra).

99457: 20-Minute Time Gate

20 cumulative minutes of care team review + patient interaction per month. Requires real-time stopwatch tracking in EHR, isolated from standard E/M visit time. All handled by Centralized Care Hub — zero burden on clinic floor staff.

Apple Watch Eligibility: Series 9+ with FDA 510(k) clearances for ECG, AFib detection, SpO2, and heart rate. Staff must document the specific watch model during enrollment. Zero hardware cost to the clinic.
